# Diet Swad - Natural Energy Bites

A modern, responsive website for Diet Swad, featuring natural energy bites made from ancient Indian superfoods.

## Overview

Diet Swad offers 100% natural energy bites crafted from only 5 simple ingredients: Arabian dates, almonds, pumpkin seeds, peanut butter, and pure desi ghee. With no preservatives or added sugar, these power bites provide 10g of protein per serving.

## Features

- **Responsive Design**: Fully optimized for desktop, tablet, and mobile devices

- **Product Showcase**: Detailed product cards with pricing and features
