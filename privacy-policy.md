### 5.1 Types of Cookies We Use

| **Cookie Type** | **Purpose** | **Duration** | **Can You Disable?** | **Impact of Disabling** |

| **Functional Cookies** | Remember your preferences (language, previous searches) | 1 year | ‚úÖ Yes | Will need to re-enter preferences each visit |

### 5.2 Specific Cookies We Set

**Meta Pixel Cookies (Meta Platforms Ireland Limited):**
- **_fbp (Facebook Browser Pixel):** Identifies your browser for ad attribution (90 days)
- **_fbc (Facebook Click ID):** Stores information about Facebook/Instagram ad clicks (90 days)
- **fr:** Delivers and measures Meta ads (90 days)

**Google Analytics Cookies (Google Ireland Limited):**
- **_ga:** Distinguishes unique visitors (2 years)
- **_gid:** Distinguishes users (24 hours)
- **_gat:** Throttles request rate (1 minute)

**Session Cookies:**
- **PHPSESSID or similar:** Maintains your session (deleted when browser closes)
- **cart_token:** Remembers items in shopping cart

### 5.3 How to Manage Cookies

**Browser Controls:**

**Chrome:**

**Safari (iOS/Mac):**
1. Go to Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Manage Website Data
2. Select "Remove All" or choose specific sites
3. Enable "Prevent cross-site tracking"

**Firefox:**
1. Go to Options ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Cookies and Site Data
2. Choose "Delete cookies when Firefox is closed"
3. Enable "Enhanced Tracking Protection"

**Note:** Blocking essential cookies may prevent you from placing orders or using site features.

## 13. Do Not Track (DNT) Signals and Browser Privacy Settings

### 13.1 Current DNT Support

Our website does **not currently respond to Do Not Track (DNT)** browser signals.

**Reason:** There is no universal industry standard for how websites should interpret and respond to DNT signals. Different browsers implement DNT differently, and advertising platforms (Meta, Google) don't honor DNT for their tracking pixels.

### 13.2 Alternative Privacy Controls Available to You

**Browser-Level Controls:**

**Cookie Settings:**
- Block third-party cookies: Prevents cross-site tracking
- Block all cookies: Maximum privacy but breaks site functionality
- Delete cookies regularly: Clears tracking history

**Private/Incognito Browsing:**
- Doesn't save browsing history or cookies
- Limits tracking across sessions
- **Note:** Websites can still track you within a single private browsing session

**Tracking Prevention Features:**
- **Safari:** Intelligent Tracking Prevention (ITP)
- **Firefox:** Enhanced Tracking Protection
- **Brave Browser:** Built-in ad and tracker blocking
- **Edge:** Tracking prevention (Balanced or Strict mode)

**Privacy-Focused Browsers:**
- Brave: Blocks ads and trackers by default
- Firefox Focus: Mobile browser designed for privacy
- DuckDuckGo Browser: Blocks hidden trackers

### 13.3 Browser Extensions for Enhanced Privacy

**Tracker Blockers:**
- **Privacy Badger** (EFF): Learns and blocks invisible trackers
- **uBlock Origin:** Blocks ads and trackers
- **Ghostery:** Shows and blocks trackers on each page

**Ad Blockers:**
- **AdBlock Plus**
- **AdGuard**

**Cookie Management:**
- **Cookie AutoDelete:** Automatically deletes cookies when tabs close
- **EditThisCookie:** View and manage cookies easily

**Facebook-Specific:**
- **Facebook Container (Firefox):** Isolates Facebook tracking from rest of browsing

### 13.4 Platform-Level Opt-Outs

**Meta (Facebook/Instagram):**
- [Facebook Ad Preferences](https://www.facebook.com/adpreferences)
- Turn off "Ads based on data from partners"
- Turn off "Ads based on your activity on Facebook"

**Google:**
- [Google Ad Settings](https://adssettings.google.com)
- Turn off "Ad Personalization"
- Opt out of Google Analytics: [Google Analytics Opt-out](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

**Industry-Wide Opt-Outs:**
- [Digital Advertising Alliance (DAA)](https://optout.aboutads.info/)
- [Network Advertising Initiative (NAI)](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)
